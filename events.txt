John Smith purchased auto insurance on January 15, 2023, with a premium of $1,200 annually. The policy covers comprehensive and collision with a $500 deductible.

On February 1, 2023, John made his first premium payment of $100 monthly installment.

On March 10, 2023, John was involved in a minor car accident in the parking lot. He immediately reported the incident to his insurance company on March 11, 2023.

The claim was officially filed on March 12, 2023, with claim number CL-2023-0045.

An adjuster was assigned to the case and visited the scene on March 15, 2023. The adjuster took photos and interviewed witnesses.

The investigation began on March 16, 2023, to determine fault and assess damages.

On April 2, 2023, the investigation was completed. The insurance company determined that John was not at fault for the accident.

The claim was approved on April 5, 2023, and a settlement check for $2,350 was issued to cover the vehicle repairs.

John's policy was automatically renewed on January 15, 2024, with a slight premium increase to $1,250 annually due to the previous claim.
